================================================================
  BetterCivilization Balance Patch
  Full Changelog vs. Base Game
================================================================

This section summarizes ALL balance changes introduced by the
BetterCivilization mod across all versions relative to vanilla
Brave New World. Bug fixes are listed separately.

--- CIVILIZATIONS ---

[AZTEC]
• Sacrificial Captives: on killing a unit, earn Gold equal to
  that unit's Combat Strength (in addition to Culture).

[CHINA]
• Art of War: +15% City Growth.
• Every Farm improvement provides +1 Food for Chinese cities.
• Every food building (Granary, Watermill, Aqueduct, Hospital,
  Lighthouse, Barn) provides +1 Food for Chinese cities.

[ASSYRIA]
• Slayer of Tiamat: +15% Combat Strength when fighting on tiles
  owned by a major-civ enemy (does not apply vs. city-states or
  barbarians).
• Royal Library now also provides +1 Culture.

[DENMARK]
• Viking Fury: naval units can enter impassable ice tiles.
• Viking Fury: trade routes can be routed through ice tiles
  (ocean navigation required).
• Snow tiles yield +1 Food, +1 Production, and +1 Faith for
  Danish-owned cities.
• Tundra tiles yield +1 Production for Danish-owned cities.

[BYZANTIUM]
• Patriarch of Constantinople: +1 Faith per Great Work
  specialist (Writer, Artist, Musician, Scientist, Merchant,
  Engineer) working in any city.
• Patriarch of Constantinople: +3 Faith from Great Person tile
  improvements (Landmark, Academy, Customs House, Manufactory,
  Citadel).
• Hippodrome: now also provides +1 Food.

[FRANCE]
• Chateau improvement: the +1 Culture / +2 Gold bonus now
  unlocks at Architecture (Medieval era) instead of Flight
  (Modern era).

[EGYPT]
• Burial Tomb: now provides +10% Gold yield in the city.

[CELTS]
• Helvetic Jewellery Workshop: now also provides +2 Faith.

[VENICE]
• Serenissima: maximum Trade Route capacity is doubled.
• Venetian Trade Routes cannot be plundered by enemy units.

[HUNS]
• Scourge of God: Horse resource tiles yield +1 Food,
  +1 Production, and +1 Gold when worked by a city.

--- SOCIAL POLICIES ---

[MERITOCRACY]
• Each city causes 10% less unhappiness from city count
  (previously no bonus).

[SCHOLASTICISM]
• Now grants +5% Culture for every City-State ally
  (replaces the old +5% Science diplomat bonus; the diplomat
  culture bonus has been removed).

[RATIONALISM FINISHER]
• Grants +2 Science per improved Strategic Resource.

--- UNITS & COMBAT ---

[ALL ARCHER-CLASS UNITS]
  Applies to: Archer, Composite Bowman, Crossbowman,
  Chu-Ko-Nu, Longbowman, Bowman, Atlatlist, Machine Gun,
  Gatling Gun, Bazooka, and Barbarian equivalents.

• −33% Combat Strength when attacking a City.
• −33% Combat Strength when attacking a Siege unit.
