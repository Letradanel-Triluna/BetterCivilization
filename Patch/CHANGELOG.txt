## v0.2 - 2026-04-26

### Civilizations

**Aztec - Gold from Kills**
- Sacrificial Captives now grants gold equal to the killed unit's Combat Strength on kill.

**China - Growth & Farms**
- Art of War trait gives +15% city growth.
- Every Farm improvement provides +1 Food.
- All food buildings (Granary, Watermill, Aqueduct, Hospital, Lighthouse, Barn) each provide +1 Food.

**Assyria - Combat on Enemy Soil**
- Slayer of Tiamat grants +15% Combat Strength when fighting on tiles owned by a major-civ enemy (not city-states or barbarians).
- Royal Library now also provides +1 Culture.

**Denmark - Terrain Yields & Naval Ice**
- Viking Fury now allows naval units to enter impassable ice tiles.
- Snow tiles now yield +1 Food, +1 Production, and +1 Faith for Danish cities.
- Tundra tiles now yield +1 Production for Danish cities.

**Byzantium - Faith Rework**
- Patriarch of Constantinople grants +1 Faith per Great Work specialist (Writer, Artist, Musician, Scientist, Merchant, Engineer).
- Also grants +3 Faith from Great Work tile improvements (Landmark, Academy, Customs House, Manufactory, Citadel).
- Hippodrome now provides +1 Food.

**France - Chateau Rework**
- Chateau improvement now unlocks its +1 Culture and +2 Gold bonus at Architecture (was: Flight). Effective from the Medieval era instead of the Modern era.

**Egypt - Burial Tomb Buff**
- Burial Tomb now provides +10 Gold.

**Celts - Jewellery Workshop Buff**
- Helvetic Jewellery Workshop now also provides +2 Faith.

**Venice - Serenissima Rework**
- Trade route capacity is doubled.
- Venetian Caravans cannot be plundered.

**Portugal - Torre de Belem (Bug Fix)**
- Fixed: Torre de Belem now correctly grants +1 Happiness and +10% defensive strength to coastal cities.

**Indonesia - Spice Islanders (Bug Fix)**
- Fixed wonder happiness calculation bug.
- Now grants +1 Happiness from every luxury resource you own.

### Policies

**Meritocracy**
- Each city now causes 10% less unhappiness from city count.

**Scholasticism**
- Now grants +5% Culture for every City-State ally.

**Rationalism Finisher**
- Now grants +2 Science per improved Strategic Resource.

### Units & Combat

**Archers - Penalty vs. Cities and Siege**
- All Archer-class units (Archer, Composite Bowman, etc.) deal -33% Combat Strength when attacking a city or siege unit.

--- BALANCE CHANGES ---

[HUNS]
• +1 Food, +1 Production, +1 Gold on Horse resource tiles.
  Bonus activates when the Horse tile is worked by a city —
  which happens naturally after researching Animal Husbandry
  (the tech that reveals horses on the map).

[VENICE]
• Maximum number of Trade Routes doubled (+100% to Trade Route cap).
• Venice Trade Routes cannot be pillaged by enemy units.

[PORTUGAL — Torre de Belem]
• Happiness bonus increased from +1 to +2.
• Provides enhanced City Defense in the city where it is built
  (local bonus, not global).

[ALL ARCHER-CLASS UNITS]
  Applies to: Archer, Composite Bowman, Crossbowman,
  Chu-Ko-Nu, Longbowman, Bowman, Atlatlist, Machine Gun,
  Gatling Gun, Bazooka, and Barbarian equivalents.

• −33% Combat Strength when attacking Cities.
• −33% Combat Strength when attacking Siege units.

--- BUG FIXES ---

• Fixed Portugal's civilization description incorrectly
  displaying Venice's "Trade Routes cannot be pillaged" text.
  Mare Clausum now shows the correct description.
