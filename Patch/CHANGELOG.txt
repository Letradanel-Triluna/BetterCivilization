## v0.2 - 2026-04-26

### New Civilizations Features

**Aztec - Gold from Kills**
- Sacrificial Captives trait now grants gold equal to the unit's combat strength on kill (e.g. killing a strength-30 unit yields 30 gold).

**China - Growth & Farms**
- The Art of War trait now adds +15% city growth and +1 food to every Farm improvement.

**Assyria - Combat on Enemy Soil**
- The Slayer of Tiamat trait grants +15% combat strength when fighting in tiles owned by a major-civ enemy (does not apply to city-states or barbarians).

**Denmark - Naval Movement Through Ice**
- The Viking Fury trait now allows naval units to enter impassable ice tiles (Arctic exploration without needing the Astronomy technology workaround).

**Byzantium - Faith Generation Rework**
- The Patriarch of Constantinople trait now generates +1 Faith for every Great Work specialist (Writer, Artist, Musician, Scientist, Merchant, Engineer).
- Also grants +3 Faith for Great Work tile improvements (Landmark, Academy, Customs House, Manufactory, Citadel).
- Hippodrome now provides +1 Food in addition to its existing bonuses.

### Policy & Building Tweaks

**Scholasticism** - Now grants +5% Culture for every City-State you are an ally of (in addition to its existing science bonus).

**Rationalism Finisher** - Now grants +2 Science per improved Strategic Resource (Horses, Iron, Coal, Oil, Aluminum, Uranium) in addition to +1 Happiness.

### Rebalance

**Archer - Penalty vs. Cities and Siege Units**
- Archers (and their upgrades, Composite Bowmen) now suffer a -33% combat penalty when attacking cities or siege units. This prevents the early-game archer-rush from being disproportionately dominant.

### Bug Fixes

**Venice - Trade Route Bug Fixed**
- Trade routes from Venice were incorrectly able to target occupied cities (puppet-city slots from Merchant of Venice). Fixed.

**Venice - Pillage Immunity Bug Fixed**
- Venice's Merchant of Venice units could incorrectly pillage certain improvements. Fixed.

**Portugal - Torre de Belem**
- Fixed a bug where the Torre de Belem wonder was not providing its intended +1 Happiness and +10% Defense bonus to coastal cities.

**Indonesia - Wonder Happiness**
- Fixed a crash/incorrect-value bug that occurred when Indonesia's unique wonders applied their happiness bonuses.

--- BALANCE CHANGES ---

[HUNS]
• +1 Food, +1 Production, +1 Gold on Horse resource tiles.
  Bonus activates when the Horse tile is worked by a city —
  which happens naturally after researching Animal Husbandry
  (the tech that reveals horses on the map).

[VENICE]
• Maximum number of Trade Routes doubled (+100% to Trade Route cap).
• Venice Trade Routes cannot be pillaged by enemy units.

[PORTUGAL — Torre de Belem]
• Happiness bonus increased from +1 to +2.
• Provides enhanced City Defense in the city where it is built
  (local bonus, not global).

[ALL ARCHER-CLASS UNITS]
  Applies to: Archer, Composite Bowman, Crossbowman,
  Chu-Ko-Nu, Longbowman, Bowman, Atlatlist, Machine Gun,
  Gatling Gun, Bazooka, and Barbarian equivalents.

• −33% Combat Strength when attacking Cities.
• −33% Combat Strength when attacking Siege units.

--- BUG FIXES ---

• Fixed Portugal's civilization description incorrectly
  displaying Venice's "Trade Routes cannot be pillaged" text.
  Mare Clausum now shows the correct description.
